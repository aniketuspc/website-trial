Paragraph 1

A paragraph is a distinct section of writing that typically focuses on a single idea or topic, usually indicated by an indent at the beginning. It's a group of sentences organized to convey a central point and help readers follow the flow of ideas. Paragraphs are essential for organizing longer pieces of writing, making it easier to understand and follow the author's thoughts.
